Vacation like a Local

Forget Traveling like a Tourist

Let's face it, traveling while abroad can get expensive and most touring companies are booked. There are times when you have to either cancel or change the date of the excursion because over-crowding is a facotr in some destinations. That is why I am offering a way for locals and tourist to enter into a mutually benefecial contract. Phones will be provided for locals to get in contact with tourist that are looking for a ride or an escursion last minute.

  • How it works?
    1. Download the TourDifferent App
    2. Search by category such as ride or Excursion
    3. After locating a match, use the messaging feature to negotiate pricing.
  • Is it Safe?
    • Every local will go through a screening proces.
    • Includes a background check with local and international officials.
    • After screening is complete, they will have in-person interview.
  • Why try it?
    • You can beat the normal schedules and time constraints.
    • Experience local culture beyond your resort.
    • Give your traveling companion's memories they won't forget.
